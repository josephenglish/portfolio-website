Games on Steam

Galaxy Cat


The first game I put out on Steam, and one of my earliest game projects.


Play as Coz the Cosmic Kitten as you travel the solar system reconnecting the universe together.


While there are many aspects of Galaxy Cat that I would do differently now, I am proud of what I was able to accomplish back in 2020.

Escape the Underground


This was the second game I released on Steam.


At the bottom of a massive underground dump, a sentient pile of sludge is born. After finding a discarded book with photos of the surface, they decide journey upward.


Escape the Underground was supposed to be a short project that ended up growing into a full game.
